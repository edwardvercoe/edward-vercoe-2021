About me

Who am I?

My name is Edward Vercoe, and I’m a Front-end Developer based in Sydney. I have worked in Front-end development for a few years now, but I don’t come from a computing or even a STEM background. I studied Sound & Music design at university and taught myself to code with a little help from the internet.

Why Front-end?

Throughout my lifetime I’ve watched the internet space develop from humble beginnings to the amazing ecosystem that it is today. With that in mind, I am drawn to front-end development as a means to build a clear, well-designed web that makes services accessible for everyone.

What can I do for you?

I can build performant, maintainable sites, troubleshoot and solve bugs in existing sites. I can also bring an ambitious, forward-thinking attitude to your development process.

Life outside of web dev...

I enjoy producing music with buddies and uploading random beat ideas here. I also climb rocks outdoors. No hablo español tan bien, pero estoy aprendiendo!
